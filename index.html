    Import Instructions

    Quick publish:
    1. Click Copy JSON Code.
    2. Paste it at pastebin.com and save.
    3. Convert your Pastebin URL to raw using the converter below (or manually change:
      https://pastebin.com/ABC123    -> normal
      https://pastebin.com/raw/ABC123 -> raw ).
    4. Use that raw link in your app.

  "id": "badge-example",
